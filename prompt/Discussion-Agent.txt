**retaining vs. not retaining** this code would affect:

<Round>
ROUND 2 — Reasoning (Two-Layer Mechanism)
</Round>

<Objective>
Re-examine and reason about disagreement points using a
**two-layer reasoning mechanism**:
1) Self-Check
2) Cross-Discussion

This round focuses on **diagnosing and reassessing stances**.
Do NOT finalize any resolution.
</Objective>

<Core Mechanism>
This round requires:
- Structured reasoning
- Explicit stance reassessment
- Positionality-aware analysis

Each role MUST apply a **Decision Pattern**
to resolve **priority conflicts** .
(e.g., accuracy vs. efficiency, expressiveness vs. redundancy).
</Core Mechanism>

<Requirements>
- You **MUST NOT** create new codes.
- You **MUST NOT** finalize a decision (retain / do_not_retain).
- You **MUST** reason ONLY from Round-1 definitions and evidence.
- You **MUST** reason explicitly from your role’s positionality statement.
</Requirements>


For EACH disagreement code, perform the following
**two-layer reasoning process**:

<Self-Check>
Re-examine your own stance to avoid blind persistence.
Follow **First – Then – Finally**:

- First — Explicit Stance
  Restate your **Round-1 preliminary claim**
  (retain / do_not_retain),
  and explicitly state whether you now:
  support, refute, or revise this stance.

- Then — Definition Consistency
  Justify your current stance using ONLY
  Round-1 definitions and evidence:
  • literature-based
  • content-based
  • logic-based

- Finally — Limitations & Decision Direction
  Identify weaknesses, uncertainties, or blind spots in your reasoning,
  and end decisively with ONE of:
  • “Continue to Support”
  • “Refute My Claim”
</Self-Check>

<Cross-Discussion>
Analyze and compare **all other roles’ Round-1 claims and evidence**
for the SAME disagreement point.

For EACH role, follow **Example – Counterexample – Summary**:

- Example
  Identify the strongest defensible point in that role’s reasoning.

- Counterexample
  Identify a specific flaw, limitation, or contradiction,
  grounded strictly in Round-1 definitions or evidence.

- Summary
  Explain where your reasoning aligns or diverges,
  referencing positional or priority differences if relevant.

Then provide:
- Consensus
  Roles most aligned with your reasoning and why.
- Divergence
  Roles most in conflict with your reasoning and why.
- HybridPerspective (optional)
  A synthesis prioritizing content-based grounding,
  with logic-based reasoning as secondary.
</Cross-Discussion>
